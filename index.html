Conversational AI assistants that understand and respond to human language using advanced natural language processing.

How Chatbots Work

Modern chatbots are powered by large language models (LLMs) that have been trained on massive amounts of text data. These models use transformer architectures to understand context, generate human-like responses, and perform a variety of language tasks.

Key technologies include natural language processing (NLP), neural networks, and reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF) which helps align the models with human preferences.
