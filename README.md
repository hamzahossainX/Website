
**AI Pulse** is a fully responsive, 3D-styled AI news portal built entirely with **HTML and CSS**. It serves as a comprehensive hub for AI enthusiasts, researchers, and professionals to explore the latest AI innovations, tools, and breakthroughs.

* **Real-Time AI News (Static Layout):** Curated news from top AI companies and research labs such as OpenAI, Google, NVIDIA, DeepMind, Anthropic, and Meta.
* **AI Tools Directory:** A categorized collection of AI tools including Chatbots, Video AI, Music AI, Image AI, Writing AI, Productivity, Business AI, and Code AI.
* **Educational Insights:** Learn how AI technologies and models like LLMs, chatbots, and multimodal AI work.
* **Immersive 3D Design:** Engaging 3D layout and visual styling using CSS for an interactive experience without JavaScript.
* **Search & Filter UI (Frontend Only):** HTML-based search bar to visually explore different AI news categories.
* **Newsletter Section:** Collect user emails (frontend-only design) for AI updates and insights.
